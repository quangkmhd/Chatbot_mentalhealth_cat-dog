About Pet Health

What is Pet Health?

Pet Health is an AI-powered assistant designed to provide information and advice about pets, particularly dogs and cats. Using advanced natural language processing and a comprehensive database of pet-related information, our chatbot can answer questions about pet care, health issues, training, and more.

How It Works

Pet Health uses a technique called Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) to provide accurate and relevant information:

  1. Data Collection: We've collected and processed information from reliable pet care resources, including PetMart.
  2. Vector Database: This information is stored in a vector database (LanceDB) that allows for semantic search.
  3. Embedding: When you ask a question, it's converted into a mathematical representation (embedding) using a multilingual model.
  4. Retrieval: The system finds the most relevant information in the database based on your question.
  5. Generation: A large language model (Llama-3.3-70b) uses the retrieved information to generate a comprehensive answer.

Use Cases

Pet Health can help with:

  • Answering questions about pet health and symptoms
  • Providing information about different dog and cat breeds
  • Offering advice on pet care, training, and behavior
  • Suggesting solutions for common pet problems

Note: While Pet Health provides helpful information, it is not a substitute for professional veterinary advice. Always consult a veterinarian for serious health concerns.

How to Use

Using Pet Health is simple:

  1. Type your question in the chat input box
  2. Press Enter or click the send button
  3. Review the response and the reference information provided
  4. Ask follow-up questions as needed

For best results, be specific in your questions and provide relevant details about your pet's situation.
